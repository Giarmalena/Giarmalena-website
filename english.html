And now we would like to tell you a story...

Giarmalena is friendship. It's the human need to translate sensation, emotions and dream into music... Thats why we want to tell you the story of four guys from Brescia, Italy, they were old friends from highscool and the just wanted to express themselves. That's how this project was born, in the most simple way: the attempt to express life through sounds. The biggest opportunity that this gives us it's the possibility to create new connections between us as a group of friend, and between us and the world. Nothing compares to the feeling you get when you can communicate your own universe whith music. We put all our efforts and ourselves in this attempt, even in our name, try to look for us!

(Gia)como Ferrari, voice and guitar

Hi! I'm Giacomo and I would like to tell you a few things about who I am, what I do and why I got here. When I was a kid, the only way I knew to speak about how I felt was to write down pages and pages full of words, words in the form of metaphors, of big feelings expressed through simbolisms. This is how my lyrics were born, as my only way to truly understand myself; years passed, I took some guitar lessons and combined that with my writings, so that they became music. This is how they became a tool not only for my introspection, but also to communicate to others, and the biggest statisfaction that this passion gives me is to see other people share my feeling through my music.

Angelo A(rma)nti, guitar

Music has always been in my life. It's a passion my mother, who's a musician, gave me, and that I always shared with her: I can remember as if it was yesterday the long evenings whe spent together playing instruments! Since I was only a small kid i used to attend to music school, where i learnt to play piano and classic guitar. Those were truly impotrant years for me and my training, but in my teenage years I startet to feel the urge to break the mould, so I started to learn about electric guitar and musical improv. In this way guitar sounds really became for me something as close as speaking, something that allows me to instantly communicate my feelings.

Miche(le) Longhena, drums

Hi guys! My name is Michele and I want to tell you how Giarmalena were born. I started to play drums when I was a kid and alongside with my brother Daniele we would spend evenings making the neighbours crazy. In highschool I met Angelo and Giacomo, that shared my passion for music, I brought them home to meet my brother and we would play together sometimes. Only years later, when we grown up, we started to give that a name and to meet regularly: this is how we came up with this project, that somehow has always been part of us.

Daniele Longhe(na), bass

Hi, nice to meet you, my name is Daniele, I was born in 1989 and I work as an electrician. As I kid I was always angry with the whole world, and music has always been my relif: I teached myself to play bass, and then I convinced my parents to let me take lessons. The most beautifoul days of my childhood were the ones spent playing whit my brother Michele, we were two small kids from the countryside that found fun and happiness with music. I belive that starting this project with my friends really give a boost to my creativity, and also transformed what alredy was a beautiful friendship to a even closer relationship; it truly looks incredible to me how much we get ahead.
